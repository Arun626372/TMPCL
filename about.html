About TMPCL

TMPCL is a movement to discover, develop and showcase tennis ball cricket talent from every corner of Madhya Pradesh.

🎯

Our Mission

Discover raw talent and provide a professional platform.

👁️

Our Vision

Become India's respected tennis ball cricket league.

⭐

Our Values

Integrity, passion, teamwork and excellence.

Why TMPCL Was Created

Madhya Pradesh has many talented players, but many do not get the right platform. TMPCL is created to give fair trials, competition and recognition.
