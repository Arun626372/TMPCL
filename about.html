About TMPCL

TMPCL is a movement to discover, develop and showcase tennis ball cricket talent from every corner of Madhya Pradesh.

🎯

Mission

Discover and nurture raw tennis ball cricket talent.

👁

Vision

Become a respected tennis ball cricket platform.

🤝

Purpose

Unite communities and promote healthy competition.

⭐

Values

Integrity, passion, respect and excellence.

Why TMPCL Was Created

Madhya Pradesh has huge cricket talent. TMPCL gives local players a professional platform, trial opportunity, team selection structure and league recognition.
